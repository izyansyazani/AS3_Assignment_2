Chicken Katsu Curry
Description: Crispy breaded chicken served with a rich Japanese curry sauce. Calories: 450 kcal 2 boneless chicken breasts 1 cup panko breadcrumbs 1 egg, beaten 2 cups cooked white rice 1 onion, sliced 2 carrots, chopped 2 potatoes, diced 3 cups chicken broth 2 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dip each chicken breast in the beaten egg, then coat with panko breadcrumbs. 2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove and set aside. 3. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and sauté the onion, carrots, and potatoes until softened. 4. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are tender. 5. Slice the fried chicken and serve with the vegetable curry over cooked white rice.
Chicken Tikka Masala
Description: Tender chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled and served in a creamy tomato sauce. Calories: 480 kcal 2 boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes 1 cup plain yogurt 3 tbsp tikka masala spice mix 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes 1 cup heavy cream 2 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine yogurt and tikka masala spice mix. Add chicken cubes and marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator. 2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent. 3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until browned on all sides. 4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15-20 minutes. 5. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes until heated through. 6. Serve hot with naan bread or rice.
Beef Shawarma Wraps
Description: Thinly sliced marinated beef, wrapped in pita bread with fresh veggies and tahini sauce. Calories: 520 kcal 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced 4 pita breads 1 cup plain yogurt 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 lemon, juiced 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp paprika 1 cucumber, thinly sliced 1 tomato, diced 1/2 cup tahini sauce Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, cumin, and paprika. Add beef slices and marinate for at least 1 hour. 2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated beef slices until browned and cooked through. 3. Warm the pita breads in the oven or microwave. 4. Spread tahini sauce on each pita bread. Add cooked beef slices, cucumber slices, and diced tomatoes. 5. Roll up the pita breads tightly and serve.
Chicken Fajitas
Description: Grilled chicken strips, bell peppers, and onions, served with tortillas, salsa, and sour cream. Calories: 420 kcal 2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced 2 bell peppers, sliced 1 onion, sliced 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tbsp chili powder 1 tsp cumin 1/2 tsp garlic powder 1/2 tsp onion powder 4 flour tortillas 1 cup salsa 1/2 cup sour cream Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and onion powder. Add chicken slices and marinate for 30 minutes. 2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken slices until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside. 3. In the same skillet, add more olive oil if needed and sauté bell peppers and onions until tender. 4. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss to combine with the bell peppers and onions. 5. Serve the chicken fajita mixture with warm flour tortillas, salsa, and sour cream.
Mediterranean Grilled Lamb Chops
Description: Tender lamb chops marinated in olive oil, garlic, and herbs, grilled to perfection. Calories: 480 kcal 4 lamb chops 1/4 cup olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp rosemary 1 tsp thyme 1 tsp oregano Salt and pepper, to taste Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add lamb chops and marinate for at least 1 hour. 2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Grill the lamb chops for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare or to desired doneness. 3. Remove lamb chops from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving. 4. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.
Beef Bulgogi
Description: Thinly sliced beef marinated in a sweet and savory sauce, grilled to perfection. Calories: 500 kcal 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tbsp brown sugar 2 tbsp sesame oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp ginger, minced 2 green onions, thinly sliced Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and minced ginger. 2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade and let it marinate for at least 1 hour. 3. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the marinated beef slices for 2-3 minutes per side or until cooked through. 4. Garnish with thinly sliced green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
Thai Green Curry with Chicken
Description: Creamy and aromatic Thai green curry with tender chicken, bamboo shoots, and bell peppers. Calories: 480 kcal 2 boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk 2 tbsp green curry paste 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1/2 cup bamboo shoots 2 tbsp fish sauce 1 tbsp brown sugar 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves Instructions: 1. In a pan, heat half of the coconut milk over medium heat. Add green curry paste and stir until fragrant. 2. Add chicken cubes to the pan and cook until they are no longer pink. 3. Add the remaining coconut milk, sliced red bell pepper, bamboo shoots, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir well and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes. 4. Remove from heat and stir in fresh basil leaves. 5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
Chicken Shawarma Platter
Description: Grilled marinated chicken served with garlic sauce, pita bread, and a variety of toppings. Calories: 550 kcal 2 boneless chicken breasts 1/4 cup olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp paprika 1/2 tsp turmeric 1/4 cup yogurt 4 pita breads 1 cup garlic sauce 1 cucumber, sliced 1 tomato, sliced 1/2 cup pickled turnips Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, and yogurt to make the marinade. 2. Add chicken breasts to the marinade and let it marinate for at least 2 hours. 3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Grill the marinated chicken breasts until they are fully cooked. 4. Slice the grilled chicken into strips. 5. Warm the pita breads and spread garlic sauce on each pita. 6. Place grilled chicken strips, cucumber slices, tomato slices, and pickled turnips on the pita breads. 7. Roll up the pita breads tightly and serve.
Fish Tacos
Description: Crispy fish fillets served in warm tortillas with cabbage slaw and a creamy sauce. Calories: 420 kcal 4 fish fillets (such as tilapia or cod) 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 tsp salt 1/2 tsp black pepper 1/2 tsp paprika 1/2 cup milk 1 egg 1 cup shredded cabbage 1/4 cup mayonnaise 1 tbsp lime juice 1 tsp hot sauce (optional) 4 flour tortillas Instructions: 1. In a shallow dish, mix together flour, salt, pepper, and paprika. 2. In another dish, whisk together milk and egg. 3. Dip each fish fillet into the milk mixture, then dredge in the flour mixture, coating both sides. 4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the fish fillets for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden and crispy. 5. In a bowl, combine shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, lime juice, and hot sauce to make the slaw. 6. Warm the flour tortillas. Place a fried fish fillet on each tortilla and top with cabbage slaw. 7. Serve hot with additional lime wedges and hot sauce if desired.
Lentil Soup (Shorbat Adas)
Description: A hearty and nutritious soup made with lentils, vegetables, and Middle Eastern spices. Calories: 350 kcal 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed 1 onion, chopped 2 carrots, chopped 2 celery stalks, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 6 cups vegetable broth 1 tsp ground cumin 1/2 tsp ground turmeric 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon Salt and pepper, to taste 2 tbsp olive oil 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped Instructions: 1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until vegetables are softened. 2. Add minced garlic, ground cumin, ground turmeric, and ground cinnamon. Stir and cook for another minute. 3. Add rinsed lentils and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until lentils are tender. 4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley.

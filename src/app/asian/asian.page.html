Indonesian Soto Ayam
Description: A fragrant Indonesian chicken soup with rich spices. Calories: 450 kcal 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised 3 kaffir lime leaves 3 cloves garlic 2-inch ginger, sliced 2 tsp turmeric powder 1 tsp coriander seeds 1 tsp cumin seeds 3 cups chicken broth 2 tbsp vegetable oil Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: 1. In a pot, heat vegetable oil and sauté garlic, ginger, lemongrass, and spices. 2. Add chicken pieces and brown them. 3. Add chicken broth, kaffir lime leaves, salt, and pepper. Simmer until chicken is tender. 4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
Coconut Rice Balls (Klepon)
Description: Sweet Indonesian rice cakes filled with palm sugar. Calories: 220 kcal Filling: 500g grated coconut 200g palm sugar Dough: 300g glutinous rice flour 200ml warm water 1 tsp pandan extract Pinch of salt Instructions: 1. Mix grated coconut and palm sugar to make the filling. 2. Combine glutinous rice flour, warm water, pandan extract, and salt to make the dough. 3. Take a small portion of dough, flatten it, and add the coconut filling in the center. 4. Seal and shape into balls. 5. Boil the rice balls until they float. Drain and serve.
Singaporean Hainanese Chicken Rice
Description: A popular Singaporean dish of fragrant chicken served over rice. Calories: 380 kcal 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup jasmine rice 3 cups chicken broth 2 cloves garlic, minced 1-inch ginger, grated 2 tbsp sesame oil 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tsp salt 1 tsp sugar 1 cucumber, sliced 2 tbsp chopped cilantro Instructions: 1. Rinse rice and cook it with chicken broth, garlic, and ginger. 2. Once cooked, fluff rice and set aside. 3. Poach chicken breasts in simmering water until cooked through. Slice thinly. 4. Serve chicken over rice, garnished with cucumber, cilantro, and a mixture of sesame oil, soy sauce, salt, and sugar.
Korean Japchae
Description: A Korean dish of stir-fried sweet potato noodles with vegetables and beef. Calories: 320 kcal 200g sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon) 200g beef rib eye, thinly sliced 1 onion, sliced 1 carrot, julienned 1 red bell pepper, sliced 2 green onions, chopped 4 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp soy sauce 2 tbsp sesame oil 1 tbsp sugar Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Boil sweet potato noodles until soft. Drain and set aside. 2. Stir-fry beef until cooked. Remove and set aside. 3. In the same pan, stir-fry garlic, onion, carrot, and bell pepper. 4. Add cooked noodles, beef, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix well. 5. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
Japanese Chicken Teriyaki
Description: Classic Japanese dish of grilled chicken glazed with teriyaki sauce. Calories: 420 kcal 500g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless 1/2 cup soy sauce 1/4 cup mirin 1/4 cup sake 2 tbsp sugar 1 tbsp vegetable oil 1 tsp grated ginger 1 clove garlic, minced Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish Instructions: 1. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant. 2. Add chicken thighs and brown them on both sides. 3. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Pour over chicken and simmer until sauce thickens. 4. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.
Thai Green Curry
Description: A spicy Thai dish made with chicken and aromatic green curry paste. Calories: 380 kcal 500g chicken breast, sliced 400ml coconut milk 3 tbsp green curry paste 1 eggplant, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 zucchini, sliced 10-12 Thai basil leaves 2 tbsp fish sauce 1 tbsp palm sugar 1 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and green curry paste. Sauté until fragrant. 2. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink. 3. Add coconut milk, eggplant, bell pepper, zucchini, fish sauce, and palm sugar. 4. Simmer until vegetables are tender and sauce thickens. 5. Stir in Thai basil leaves just before serving.
Malaysian Laksa
Description: A spicy and tangy Malaysian noodle soup with chicken and prawns. Calories: 420 kcal 200g rice vermicelli noodles 400ml coconut milk 200g chicken breast, thinly sliced 150g prawns, peeled and deveined 2 tbsp laksa paste 1 cup bean sprouts 1 hard-boiled egg, halved 1 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. In a pot, heat vegetable oil and fry laksa paste until fragrant. 2. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink. 3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer. 4. Add prawns and cook until they turn pink. 5. Cook rice vermicelli noodles according to package instructions and divide into serving bowls. 6. Ladle laksa broth, chicken, and prawns over noodles. 7. Garnish with bean sprouts and a halved hard-boiled egg before serving.
Vietnamese Pho
Description: A flavorful Vietnamese noodle soup with beef and aromatic spices. Calories: 350 kcal 500g beef brisket 200g rice noodles 2L beef broth 1 onion, sliced 3 cloves garlic, minced 1-inch ginger, grated 2 star anise 1 cinnamon stick 2 tbsp fish sauce 1 tbsp sugar 1 bunch fresh cilantro Instructions: 1. In a pot, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. 2. Add beef brisket, star anise, and cinnamon stick. Brown the beef. 3. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 1-2 hours until beef is tender. 4. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. 5. Season broth with fish sauce and sugar. 6. Place cooked rice noodles in serving bowls, top with beef slices, and ladle hot broth over. 7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
Indonesian Rendang
Description: A rich and spicy Indonesian beef stew simmered in coconut milk and spices. Calories: 480 kcal 500g beef chuck, cut into cubes 400ml coconut milk 5 shallots, sliced 4 cloves garlic, minced 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised 5 kaffir lime leaves 2 tbsp tamarind paste 1 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and sauté shallots and garlic until fragrant. 2. Add beef cubes and brown them. Add lemongrass and kaffir lime leaves. 3. Pour in coconut milk and tamarind paste. Simmer until beef is tender and sauce thickens. 4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
Thai Massaman Curry
Description: A mild and aromatic Thai curry made with chicken, potatoes, and peanuts. Calories: 410 kcal 500g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless 2 potatoes, diced 400ml coconut milk 3 tbsp Massaman curry paste 1 onion, sliced 1/2 cup roasted peanuts 2 tbsp fish sauce 1 tbsp tamarind paste 1 tbsp sugar 1 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: 1. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and fry Massaman curry paste until fragrant. 2. Add chicken thighs and brown them. Add onions and cook until translucent. 3. Pour in coconut milk, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar. Simmer until chicken is cooked through. 4. Add diced potatoes and roasted peanuts. Continue to simmer until potatoes are tender. 5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

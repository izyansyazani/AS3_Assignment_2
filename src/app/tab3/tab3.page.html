Hari Raya Satay Grilled Skewers with Peanut Sauce Total Calories(Per Skewer with Peanut Sauce) 280-400 calories Best pairings: Rice, Salad & Pickled Vegetables Skewers of marinated and grilled meat 1 cup peanut sauce 1 tablespoon soy sauce 1 tablespoon honey 1 teaspoon ground coriander 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon turmeric 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tablespoon lime juice Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Marinate by mixing soy sauce, honey, spices, garlic, lime juice, salt, and pepper 2. Coat meat skewers with marinade. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. 3. Preheat grill to medium-high. 4. Grill skewers 3-5 minutes per side until cooked through. 5. serve with peanut sauce for dipping. Beef Rendang Slow-cooked Coconut Beef Total Calories(Per Serving) 1180 -1410 calories Best pairings: Steamed ice, Roti Canai & Chapati 500g beef, cut into cubes 400ml coconut milk 4 tablespoons rendang paste 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised 4 lime leaves 2 tablespoons tamarind paste 1 tablespoon brown sugar Salt to taste Instructions: 1.Saute rendang paste in a pot for 2-3 minutes. 2. Add beef, coconut milk, lemongrass, lime leaves, tamarind paste, brown sugar, and salt. 3. Simmer on low heat for 2-3 hours until beef is tender and sauce thickens. 4. Serve hot with rice.

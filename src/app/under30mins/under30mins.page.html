Under 30 Minutes Recipes
Teriyaki Chicken Stir-Fry
Description: Quick and easy teriyaki chicken stir-fry with vegetables. Calories: 360 kcal 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce 2 tbsp vegetable oil 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 onion, sliced 1 cup broccoli florets 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp sesame seeds, for garnish Instructions: 1. In a bowl, marinate chicken slices with teriyaki sauce for 15 minutes. 2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from skillet and set aside. 3. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed. Add bell pepper, onion, and broccoli. Cook until vegetables are tender. 4. Add minced garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional 1 minute. 5. Return cooked chicken to the skillet. Stir well to combine with vegetables. 6. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds.
Spicy Tofu Stir-Fry
Description: Spicy tofu stir-fry with mixed vegetables in a savory sauce. Calories: 300 kcal 1 block firm tofu, drained and cubed 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sriracha sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil 1 bell pepper, sliced 1 cup snow peas 1 carrot, julienned 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp grated ginger Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, sriracha sauce, and sesame oil. Add tofu cubes and marinate for 15 minutes. 2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add tofu cubes and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from skillet and set aside. 3. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed. Add bell pepper, snow peas, and carrot. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp. 4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet. Cook for an additional 1 minute. 5. Return cooked tofu to the skillet. Pour in the remaining marinade and stir well to combine. 6. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
Chicken Fajitas
Description: Easy chicken fajitas with colorful peppers and onions, served with tortillas and your favorite toppings. Calories: 320 kcal 1 lb chicken breasts, sliced 2 bell peppers, sliced 1 onion, sliced 2 tbsp fajita seasoning 8 flour tortillas 1/2 cup salsa 1/4 cup sour cream 1/4 cup guacamole Instructions: 1. In a bowl, toss chicken slices with fajita seasoning until evenly coated. 2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from skillet and set aside. 3. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed. Add bell pepper and onion slices. Cook until vegetables are tender-crisp. 4. Return cooked chicken to the skillet. Stir well to combine with vegetables. 5. Serve hot with flour tortillas, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.
Egg Drop Soup
Description: Classic Chinese egg drop soup made with chicken broth, eggs, and green onions. Calories: 70 kcal 4 cups chicken broth 2 eggs, beaten 2 green onions, thinly sliced 1 tbsp cornstarch 2 tbsp water 1 tsp sesame oil Salt and white pepper to taste Instructions: 1. In a small bowl, dissolve cornstarch in water to make a slurry. 2. In a saucepan, bring chicken broth to a simmer over medium heat. 3. Slowly pour beaten eggs into the simmering broth while stirring gently with a fork to create egg ribbons. 4. Stir in the cornstarch slurry, stirring constantly until the soup thickens slightly. 5. Remove from heat and stir in sesame oil, salt, and white pepper. 6. Ladle into bowls and garnish with sliced green onions.
Quick Beef Stir-Fry with Broccoli
Description: Quick and flavorful beef stir-fry with broccoli in a savory sauce. Calories: 350 kcal 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced 1 lb broccoli florets 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tbsp oyster sauce 2 tbsp vegetable oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp grated ginger 1 tbsp cornstarch 2 tbsp water Instructions: 1. In a small bowl, dissolve cornstarch in water to make a slurry. 2. In a separate bowl, combine soy sauce and oyster sauce. 3. In a large skillet or wok, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add beef slices and stir-fry until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside. 4. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir-fry for 1 minute. 5. Add broccoli florets to the skillet and stir-fry until tender-crisp. 6. Return cooked beef to the skillet. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce mixture. 7. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens. 8. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
Caprese Salad
Description: Simple and refreshing Italian salad made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, and balsamic glaze. Calories: 220 kcal 2 large tomatoes, sliced 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves 2 tbsp balsamic glaze 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil Salt and black pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Arrange tomato slices and mozzarella cheese slices on a serving platter, alternating between them. 2. Scatter fresh basil leaves over the tomato and mozzarella slices. 3. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze over the salad. 4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. 5. Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.
Chicken Caesar Salad
Description: Classic Caesar salad with grilled chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, croutons, and creamy Caesar dressing. Calories: 380 kcal 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped 1 cup croutons 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup Caesar dressing 2 tbsp olive oil Salt and black pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Season chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. 2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove from skillet and slice. 3. In a large salad bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese. 4. Add sliced grilled chicken to the salad. 5. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to combine. 6. Serve immediately as a main course.
Shrimp Scampi
Description: Succulent shrimp sautéed in garlic, butter, white wine, and lemon juice, served over pasta. Calories: 320 kcal 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined 4 cloves garlic, minced 1/2 cup white wine 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice 4 tbsp unsalted butter 2 tbsp olive oil 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 8 oz spaghetti or linguine Salt and black pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. 2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. 3. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and cooked through, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove from skillet and set aside. 4. In the same skillet, add white wine and lemon juice. Bring to a simmer and cook until reduced by half. 5. Reduce heat to low and whisk in butter until melted and sauce is smooth. 6. Return cooked shrimp to the skillet. Add chopped parsley and cooked pasta. Toss to combine and coat everything with the sauce. 7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. 8. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
Vegetable Stir-Fry
Description: Colorful and nutritious vegetable stir-fry with a variety of vegetables in a flavorful sauce. Calories: 180 kcal 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, thinly sliced 1/2 cup broccoli florets 1/2 cup snap peas 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp oyster sauce 1 tbsp vegetable oil 1 tsp cornstarch 2 tbsp water Instructions: 1. In a small bowl, dissolve cornstarch in water to make a slurry. 2. In a separate bowl, combine soy sauce and oyster sauce. 3. In a large skillet or wok, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. 4. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for 1 minute. 5. Add bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, snap peas, and mushrooms to the skillet. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp. 6. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce mixture. 7. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens. 8. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
Mushroom Risotto
Description: Creamy and flavorful mushroom risotto made with Arborio rice, mushrooms, onions, and Parmesan cheese. Calories: 320 kcal 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice 4 cups vegetable broth 1 lb mushrooms, sliced 1 onion, finely chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup white wine 2 tbsp olive oil 2 tbsp unsalted butter 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley Salt and black pepper to taste Instructions: 1. In a saucepan, heat vegetable broth over low heat to keep it warm. 2. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil and unsalted butter over medium heat. 3. Add chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until translucent, about 2 minutes. 4. Add Arborio rice to the skillet. Stir to coat the rice with the oil and butter mixture. 5. Pour in white wine. Cook, stirring constantly, until the wine is absorbed. 6. Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet. Stir-fry until mushrooms are tender. 7. Add a ladleful of warm vegetable broth to the skillet. Cook, stirring constantly, until the broth is absorbed. 8. Continue adding warm vegetable broth, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly, until the rice is creamy and cooked through (about 20-25 minutes). 9. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley. 10. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. 11. Serve hot as a main course or side dish.

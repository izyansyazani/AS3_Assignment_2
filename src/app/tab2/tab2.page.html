Spaghetti
Description: Classic spaghetti with a rich tomato and garlic sauce, seasoned with herbs. Total Calories Per Serving: Approximately 450 calories Ingredients: 225g spaghetti 1 tbsp olive oil 3 cloves garlic 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes Dried oregano, basil, salt, pepper to taste Optional: Parmesan cheese Instructions: 1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions. 2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. 3. Add crushed tomatoes and season with dried herbs, salt, and pepper. 4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. 5. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.
Shepherd's Pie
Description: Hearty pie with a meaty base topped with creamy mashed potatoes. Total Calories Per Serving: Approximately 550 calories Ingredients: 500g ground lamb or beef Onion, Carrots, Garlic (diced) Frozen peas 2 tbsp tomato paste 1 cup broth 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce 1 tsp dried thyme Salt and pepper to taste For mashed potatoes: 4 large potatoes 1/2 cup milk, 2 tbsp butter Salt and pepper Instructions: 1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). 2. In a pan, sauté onion, carrots, and garlic until softened. 3. Add ground meat and cook until browned. 4. Stir in tomato paste, broth, Worcestershire sauce, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. 5. Simmer until thickened. 6. Boil potatoes until tender, then mash with milk, butter, salt, and pepper. 7. Spread the meat mixture in a baking dish, top with mashed potatoes. 8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden.
Buttermilk Chicken
Description: Crispy fried chicken marinated in tangy buttermilk and coated with a flavorful spice mix. Total Calories Per Serving: Approximately 400 calories Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 1 cup buttermilk 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon onion powder 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper Salt and pepper to taste Vegetable oil for frying Instructions: 1. In a bowl, marinate chicken breasts in buttermilk for at least 2 hours. 2. In another bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. 3. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. 4. Dredge marinated chicken in flour mixture. 5. Fry chicken until golden and crispy, about 12-15 minutes for breasts. 6. Drain on paper towels before serving.
Fried Chicken
Description: Crunchy and flavorful fried chicken with a blend of herbs and spices. Total Calories Per Serving: Approximately 500 calories Ingredients: 4 pieces of chicken (legs, thighs, or breasts) 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon garlic powder 1 teaspoon onion powder 1/2 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste Vegetable oil for frying Instructions: 1. In a bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. 2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. 3. Dredge chicken pieces in flour mixture. 4. Fry chicken until golden and crispy, about 12-15 minutes for thighs and breasts. 5. Drain on paper towels before serving.
Beef Curry
Description: Spicy and aromatic beef curry with tender beef pieces cooked in a rich coconut milk gravy. Total Calories Per Serving: Approximately 600 calories Ingredients: 500g beef, cut into cubes 1 large onion, finely chopped 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 thumb-sized ginger, grated 2 tablespoons curry powder 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground coriander 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste) 1 can (400ml) coconut milk 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: 1. In a pot, heat oil and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. 2. Add beef cubes and brown on all sides. 3. Stir in curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. 4. Add coconut milk and diced potatoes. 5. Season with salt and pepper. 6. Simmer on low heat for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until beef is tender. 7. Serve hot with rice or bread.
